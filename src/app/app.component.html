About

Welcome! You've stumbled across the placeholder for a future website dedicated to video games that help teach a foreign language. This website has no affiliation with any of the games on the list. It is simply a passion project!

There are two games on this list that are *not* language learning games (Backbone and Dodgeball Academia), however both games include a bilingual 'language switch' feature that allow a player to toggle between two languages during game play.

A more featured website is in the works, but for now, please enjoy this list of games.

You can join the Steam Group: https://steamcommunity.com/groups/LanguageLearningGames in the meantime!

To contact the organizers of this project, reach out to @manzZzari on Twitter please!
